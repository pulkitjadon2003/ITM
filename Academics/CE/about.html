Department Vision

“To establish a centre of excellence in the field of civil engineering for imparting technical education and conducting cutting-edge research; to serve as a valuable resource for industry and society. ”

Department Mission

  • To provide education, training, and resources to develop civil engineering technical manpower to fulfil current and future industry demands.
  • To build new knowledge by conducting cutting-edge research in continuous collaboration with industry, with a focus on the development of leadership skills in young minds.
  • To produce Civil Engineers who are ethically and morally responsible towards the society and nation.

Department of civil engineering having been formed since 2009, has already contributed significantly to the society. The department offers Bachelor of Engineering degree to the students. The department actively promotes events which enhances the technical, communicational and organizational skills.
