Research & Development Activities

A lot of emphasis is given to Research & Development in ITMGOI. The first and foremost attestation to this point is the creation of a dedicated Open Source Software Lab which is the first of its kind in a private sector institute in whole MP.

The lab contains state of the art software covering all fields of science and engineering, thereby, enabling the students and faculty members of the group to apply their theoretical knowledge in designing practical applications.

The faculty members of ITMGOI are regularly involved in publishing papers in national and international journals of high impact factors. In addition to this, the B E students are also working on the applications of theoretical ideas currently in the curriculum.

Sponsored Research Project

  • A research project entitled “Electronic and Transport Properties of Chemically Functionalized Nanostructures” under department of Physics. Sanctioned by- M.P. Council of science and Technology - Bhopal.
  • “Synthesis and Characterization of Metal Sulphides Nanoparticles via Thermal decomposition of Schiff Base Metal Complexes as Single Source Precursor” under department of chemistry. Sanctioned by- M.P. Council of science and Technology - Bhopal.
