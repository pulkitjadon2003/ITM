LITERARY CLUB

It is dedicated to students sharing literary interests. The purpose of this club is to offer a relaxing and intellectual platform for students to exchange ideas, emotions and theories on select readings through creative writing, debating and quizzing. The wall magazine is a reflection of the literary skills of the students. From poems and puzzles to articles and stories, there is a wide variety of contribution on display. The editorial board conducts regular competitions to encourage these talents.

LITERARY CLUB

ECO CLUB

The club takes care about the ecology & environment issues within the campus and suggests ways & means to reduce carbon contents responsible for so much harm & damage to the Earth’s eco-system. A road map to convert the campus into green campus is being prepared with the help of Eco Club and will be implemented within next 3 years. The recycling of books which is passed on from senior students to juniors is a good eco-friendly practice.

ECO CLUB

DANCE CLUB

The club members learn the various forms of Indian & western dances & practice to fine tune and perform. Ms Vaishnavi Agarwal from Electronics and communication received third position at national level dance event at IIT Kharagpur.

DANCE CLUB

YOGA & MEDITATION CLUB

A nice space named as “Zero Gravity” has been created within ITM Campus for meditation & yoga where any one willing to join the regular club can come & try to attain equilibrium of the mind & the body. Occasional activities to de-stress the students through Yoga & breathing exercises are also organized frequently.

YOGA & MEDITATION CLUB
