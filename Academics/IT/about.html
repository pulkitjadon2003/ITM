Department Vision

The department envisions nurturing students to become technologically proficient, research competent and socially accountable for the welfare of the society.

Department Mission

  • To provide high quality education through an effective teaching-learning process, emphasizing active participation of the students.
  • To build scientifically strong engineers to cater to the needs of industry, higher studies, research and startups.
  • To stir young minds, ingrained with professional and behavioral ethics for the betterment of the society.

TThe Information Technology course at ITM is structured to churn out professionals with technical acumen in driving automation in production processes and meeting job requirements for the modern digital world. Our curriculum is specifically designed to merge theoretical know-how with on-hand practical application, enabling students to do well in the diversified domains related to information technology and IT-enabled sectors. The IT Department has advanced laboratories with all necessary application software, compilers, and hardware. Our facilities are hybrid configured with high-speed internet and Wi-Fi enabled internet across 565 terminals for easy learning.


By offering students hands-on experience with cutting-edge technologies and platforms, we ensure they gain proficiency in both software and hardware environments. Our mission is to equip students with the essential tools and knowledge needed to excel in the fast-paced and competitive world of Information Technology.
