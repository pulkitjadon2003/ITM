MEDICAL HEALTH CARE CENTER (MHCC)

ITM GOI has an in-house modern Medical Health Care Center (MHCC) with a view to facilitate a holistic development of mind & body for an all round progress of our students. The center, thus, provides preventive, promotive as well as curative health care services with a view to ensuring sound health for all the members of ITM family is also available. A full time specialist doctor for conducting the regular health check-ups and providing medical attention when needed.MHCC has a well equipped clinic with required equipments & 5 beds for in house patients alongwith one ambulance which is available round the clock.
