NBA Accreditation

NBA accreditation refers to the recognition granted by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) in India. The NBA is an autonomous body that evaluates and accredits programs offered by technical institutions, primarily in the fields of engineering, management, pharmacy, and applied arts and crafts. The purpose of NBA accreditation is to ensure that educational programs meet certain standards of quality and relevance to industry and academia.

Institute has NBA accreditation for Computer Science Engineering(CSE) and Master of Business Administration(MBA)


AICTE

AICTE stands for the All India Council for Technical Education. It is a statutory body and a national-level council for technical education in India, under the Department of Higher Education, Ministry of Education. AICTE is responsible for planning, formulating, and regulating standards for technical education, which includes engineering, technology, management, architecture, pharmacy, and applied arts and crafts in India.


RGPV

RGPV stands for Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya (also known as Rajiv Gandhi Technological University). It is a state university located in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh, India. Established in 1998, RGPV is the premier institution for technical and engineering education in the state and is responsible for overseeing a large number of affiliated colleges.
