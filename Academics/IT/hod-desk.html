Head's Message


Dr. Aditya Vidyarthi
Professor and Head,
Department of Information Technology,
ITM Gwalior

I am very proud to welcome you all in the Department of Information Technology at Institute of Technology & Management. Our department is an abode of newfangled technological development where we try to surpass the horizons of knowledge and technology. Our goal is to inspire, educate and empower the next generation of IT leaders who will drive the industry as well make an impact on the society Our department is well equipped with ultramodern laboratories and technically advanced infrastructure to give you first-hand training in emerging technology and latest trends. Our modern facilities make you fit enough to deal with the dynamic challenges in the IT arena.

The Department has adopted an Outcome-Based Education (OBE) approach that helps in successfully preparing students for their professional careers. This educational philosophy makes our graduates be professionals who are not only equipped with technical skills but also ethics-oriented and global in their competence.

We take pride in our team of highly qualified faculty members who are not only pass out from top institutes of India, but are also passionate about teaching and research to enhance your full potential of employ ability as future IT professionals. We dedicate ourselves to inspire and guide you towards a successful career because your success is ours. we ensure you a memorable journey through our department which will enable not only to develop into responsible graduates but also world class professional in the making ready to accept challenges on this global platform.

Practical experience is offered to students and the opportunity to work first-hand in the state-of-the-art laboratory on research projects with local industry experts, collecting data and conducting research that leads to viable solutions for businesses.

Best wishes for a successful and rewarding academic journey!
