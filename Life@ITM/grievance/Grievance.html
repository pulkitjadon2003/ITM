Grievance & Ragging

Welcome to the Grievance Redressal & Anti-Ragging Portal

At ITM University, Gwalior, we are committed to providing a safe, inclusive, and supportive environment for all our students. The Grievance Redressal & Anti-Ragging Cell has been established to address student concerns and ensure a campus culture that promotes mutual respect, dignity, and academic excellence.

Grievance Redressal Mechanism

The Grievance Redressal Cell at ITM Gwalior is designed to help students voice their concerns regarding academic, administrative, or personal issues. Our primary goal is to ensure timely resolution of all grievances, fostering an environment where students can thrive academically and socially without unnecessary hurdles.

Common Grievances Addressed:

  • Academic matters (exams, evaluation, results, etc.)
  • Administrative issues
  • Infrastructure and facility-related concerns
  • Discrimination or harassment

Anti-Ragging Policy

ITM University maintains a Zero-Tolerance Policy toward ragging in any form. Ragging includes any act of physical or mental abuse, harassment, bullying, or intimidating behavior that affects the well-being of a student. ITM Gwalior is committed to providing a safe and secure learning environment.

Reporting Ragging: Students can report incidents of ragging through our helpline or the online portal. All reports will be investigated promptly, and strict actions will be taken as per the law.
