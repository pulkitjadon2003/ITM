Department Vision

“The Department of Information Technology envisions preparing technically competent problem solvers, researchers, innovators, entrepreneurs, and skilled IT professionals for the development of rural and backward areas of the country for the modern computing challenges. ”

Department Mission

  • To offer valuable education through an effective pedagogical teaching-learning process.
  • To shape technologically strong students for industry, research & higher studies.
  • To stimulate the young brain entrenched with ethical values and professional behaviors for the progress of society.

The course of information technology is designed to provide the highly skilled technique of automation involved production processes. The Department of Information Technology of ITM have well equipped laboratories with required application software, compilers & hardware including internet & Wi-Fi intranet connectivity on 565 terminals (ITM) of hybrid configuration supported by software like Oracle 9i, C++ Turbo, V.B. .Net., Windows XP and Linux 7.2, Win 98, Windows NT, Windows 2003 Server Enterprise Edition, Linux 8.0, Office XP, Norton Antivirus Corporate Edition, SQL server 2000, win proxy, Windows 2000 professional.
